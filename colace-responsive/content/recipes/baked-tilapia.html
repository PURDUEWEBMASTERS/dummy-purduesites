Crispy Baked Tilapia

Ingredients

  • 1 tilapia fillet
  • pepper
  • 2 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/3 cup seasoned breadcrumbs

Directions

Preheat oven to 500°. Season fillets lightly with pepper to taste. Dip in oil and dredge in breadcrumbs to coat. Arrange in a single layer in a lightly oiled shallow baking dish. Bake 10 minutes until golden, without turning or basting.
