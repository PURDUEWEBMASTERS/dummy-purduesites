About Purdue Pharma L.P.

Purdue Pharma L.P. is a privately held pharmaceutical company founded by physicians more than 50 years ago. We are committed to improving patients' lives in meaningful ways by providing effective therapies along with educational tools that support their proper use.

Purdue, in concert with its independent associated companies, is engaged in the research, development, production, and marketing of prescription and over-the-counter medicines and healthcare products.

We are pursuing a promising pipeline of new medications through internal research & development and strategic industry partnerships. At Purdue, we embrace our mission to find, develop, and introduce innovative medicines that meet the evolving needs of healthcare professionals, patients, and caregivers.
