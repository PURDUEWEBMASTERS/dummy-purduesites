Medical Conditions Associated with Constipation

Some medical conditions associated with constipation can include1-5 :

  • Anal fissures
    Shallow, thin, often painful ulcers of the anal canal.
  • Hemorrhoids
    Abnormal enlargements of veins in the anal area.
  • Rectocele
    Forward bulging or displacement of the rectum into the vagina.

Colace® Capsules may be able to help with occasional constipation associated with a medical condition.
